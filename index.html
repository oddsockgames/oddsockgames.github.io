Odd Sock Games is a London based independent game developer consisting of two adequately functioning humans.

Jonny Wick ▪ artist/designer
Gary Goard ▪ programmer/designer

The pair met when they were smaller versions of themselves and bonded over a love of games, which they now make together.

You are the wind. Will you aid Yuri the hedgehog?

Leaves is an upcoming experimental point and click game built in HTML5 Canvas. Once available, it should only take a few minutes to experience.

1001 robots were created to construct an elaborate subterranean structure named Labrys. With the project complete, they have been left behind with only the code inside of them to give them purpose.

SPARK is an upcoming exploratory adventure where discovery of item upgrades is vital to progression. It has a focus on puzzle solving over action, by using the environment around you in interesting ways to move yourself about. It is designed to be experienced in one sitting and should take under an hour.

See our concept art in it's early and embarrassing stages on Tumblr. Listen to some sounds on Bandcamp, doctors would probably agree that it greatly improves your overall ear health if we could find some to ask.

They say that Jonny Wick held a colouring pencil in the womb, although that may have just been a smudge on the ultrasound.

Jonny does art, design, story and sound for Odd Sock Games.

The birds and squirrels of East London affectionately know him as, 'That pervert with the zoom lens who is always taking pictures of us.'

It took Gary Goard many years to grow a beard but once he did, he and the once barren land of his face never looked back.

Gary does code, development and design for Odd Sock Games.

He is very fond of wheels, particularly when two are stuck on some metal pipes which can then be ridden about town
